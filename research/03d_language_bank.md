# 03d Language Bank — Greta’s Bakies

Supplement to `03_customer_voice.md`, `03b_customer_pain_map.md`, and `03c_social_voice_expanded.md`. Built to close ResearchQA Gap 3 with category-organized language for brand, website, social, packaging, ads, and offer copy.

## Guardrails before using this bank

- Greta’s should avoid unverified claims such as “organic,” “healthiest,” “all-natural,” “chemical-free,” or “preservative-free” unless legally and operationally verified.
- Safer ingredient stance: **ingredient-conscious**, **real butter**, **real chocolate**, **premium mix-ins**, **minimal artificial coloring**, **only Red Velvet uses added color**.
- Use “Levain-style” carefully. It is useful internally as a category reference; external copy should usually say **thick bakery-style**, **NYC-inspired thick cookies**, or **4oz dense/fudgy cookies** to avoid over-reliance on another brand.
- Do not overpromise warmth at pickup unless operationally true. Safer: **best enjoyed warm**, **baked fresh**, **reheating card included**.

## 7. Objection-handling language

### Objection: “Is it worth the price?”
- Each cookie is 4oz, baked in small batches, and boxed for gifting.
- The price reflects size, ingredients, freshness, and presentation.
- These are dessert cookies — built to feel special, not disposable.
- Compare the full box experience: weight, flavor, packaging, and freshness.

### Objection: “Will it be too sweet?”
- Rich and indulgent, but balanced with salt, chocolate, nuts, and browned/buttery notes.
- Sweet enough for dessert, balanced enough to want another bite.
- Decadent, not one-note sweet.
- For people who like their cookies rich, not just sugary.

### Objection: “Is pickup complicated?”
- Pre-order by [cutoff] and choose your pickup window.
- Pickup details are confirmed after ordering.
- Local delivery is available within [radius] when capacity allows.
- We keep windows limited so boxes stay fresh and pickups stay smooth.

### Objection: “Will it still be good later?”
- Yes — each box includes reheating guidance for the best texture.
- Warm briefly before serving for the softest center.
- Store airtight and warm before sharing.
- Best enjoyed within [timeframe], especially warm.

### Objection: “Can I gift this?”
- Yes — boxes are designed to be handed over as-is.
- Add a short note and we’ll include it in the box.
- Flavor and allergen cards are included so the box is easy to share.
- Great for host gifts, birthdays, office treats, teachers, and thank-yous.

### Objection: “Are there allergens?”
- Each box includes flavor/allergen information.
- Nut flavors are clearly labeled.
- Made in a home kitchen that also handles common allergens; cross-contact may occur.
- Please check flavor details before ordering for groups.
