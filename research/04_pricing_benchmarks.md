# 04 Pricing Benchmarks — Premium Cookie Price Comparison

## Summary
Premium cookie pricing in Greta’s competitive set spans roughly **$4-$12 per cookie**, depending on size, brand status, shipping, and packaging. The cleanest local benchmark for Greta is Dolce by Tina at **4 x 6oz cookies for $27** ($6.75 each) and Chip Cookies at **4 for $19.80** ($4.95 each) / single large flavors around **$6.30**. National shipped brands normalize higher prices: Levain’s 4-pack is **$32** ($8 each), Fat & Weird’s 4-count is **$34** ($8.50 each), and Last Crumb is often **$100-$140 per dozen** ($8.33-$11.67 each). Paderia local pricing appears lower in-store historically ($4.75 in a 2023 Reddit post) but higher on delivery platforms ($7.25 Uber Eats snippet). Because Greta’s cookies are **4oz** rather than 6oz, a recommended starting range is **$5.25-$6.25 per cookie**, with gift boxes priced to increase average order value while staying below 6oz Irvine boutique pricing.

## What pricing says about positioning
The market supports a premium price when at least three signals are visible: size/weight, flavor complexity, and gift presentation. Shipping brands charge more because they build shipping, handling, and national brand equity into price. Local pickup brands must compensate with freshness, convenience, and “made nearby” trust. Greta has a 4oz cookie, so pricing should not copy 6oz Dolce or 5.5oz Fat & Weird on a pure per-cookie basis unless packaging and brand desirability are unusually high.

## Recommended Greta pricing architecture
- **Single cookie / informal add-on:** $5.50-$6.00.  
- **4-pack tasting/gift box:** $22-$24 ($5.50-$6.00 each). This sits above Chip’s 4-pack ($19.80) but below Dolce 6oz box ($27) and Levain shipped ($32).  
- **6-pack dinner-party box:** $32-$36.  
- **12-pack party/office box:** $60-$66, with optional gift wrap/note add-on.  
- **Seasonal/limited flavors:** +$0.50-$1.00 per cookie only if ingredient cost is visibly higher (matcha/macadamia, raspberry, caramel/pretzel).  
- **Corporate/wedding bulk:** quote by count; maintain premium look but offer predictable tiers such as 24, 48, 96 cookies.

## Key pricing caution
Avoid underpricing. A $4 cookie would place Greta too close to mass-market chains and reduce perceived gift value. Avoid overpricing without proof: $7-$8 for a 4oz cookie may trigger the “expensive for a cookie” sentiment seen on Reddit/Yelp unless packaging and brand demand are strong. The sweet spot for launch is likely **$22-$24 per 4-pack**, then test elasticity.
