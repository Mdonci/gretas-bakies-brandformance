# 05 Gift Market — OC Weddings, Corporate Gifting, Holidays, and Occasions

## Executive opportunity
Greta’s Bakies should treat gifting as a core market, not a side use case. The product already fits gift occasions: cookies are shareable, relatively affordable compared with cakes/catering, easy to personalize by flavor, and emotionally warm. In Orange County, the strongest occasions are host gifts, dinner parties, birthdays, school/teacher gifts, bridal showers, wedding welcome/favor boxes, Friendsgiving, holiday boxes, and corporate/team gifting.

## OC wedding market
Wedding.report’s Orange County page reports **2025 total annual wedding expenditure of $1.041B** and average wedding cost of **$50,982**, ranking the Orange County market highly. Breezit, citing the 2024 Wedding Report, reports Orange County average wedding cost at **$49,061** and median out-of-pocket cost at **$25,943**, and lists hundreds of OC venues. National sources such as The Knot/Zola show wedding costs remain high; one 2025/2026 summary cited average guest count around **116** and average wedding costs in the low-to-mid $30Ks nationally, with OC above national averages. The exact number of OC weddings per year was not freely visible in search snippets; this is a gap. However, Wedding.report’s $1.041B expenditure divided by its $50,982 average cost implies a large local wedding market, and the user-provided working assumption of ~12K-15K annual OC weddings should be validated with a paid/local Wedding.report export before using in investor-style TAM.

## Wedding product opportunities
Cookies are well-suited for: bridal shower dessert tables, “getting ready” boxes, welcome bags for out-of-town guests, rehearsal dinner favors, late-night snack trays, and post-wedding thank-you gifts. A Class A cottage-food model can sell direct to consumers but should confirm any venue/catering resale rules before supplying through planners or venues. Best wedding SKUs for Greta: 2-cookie favor sleeves, 4-cookie couple tasting boxes, 12-cookie family gift boxes, and 48/96-cookie dessert-table orders. Packaging should include ingredient/allergen labels, flavor cards, and reheating instructions.

## Irvine corporate gifting
Irvine and OC have a strong corporate base. Orange County Business Council/LocationOC lists major employers and companies such as Clean Energy Fuels, CoreLogic, Edwards Lifesciences, FivePoint, ICU Medical, Masimo, and Skyworks. California EDD’s major-employers page lists employers including Broadcom and Edwards Lifesciences in Irvine. Irvine’s own employer landscape includes UCI, Blizzard, medical-device companies, real estate, finance, tech, and professional services. These workplaces create recurring cookie occasions: client thank-yous, employee birthdays, team lunches, sales meetings, recruiting events, office holiday gifts, and real estate closing gifts.

## Corporate gifting trends
2025 corporate gifting trend sources emphasize personalization, sustainability, local/meaningful products, and edible gifts. Clove & Twine’s 2025 corporate gifting trends highlight personalized/experiential gifts and sustainability/eco-conscious gifting. The Sweet Tooth’s 2025 corporate holiday gifting guide says sustainable edible gifts, eco-friendly packaging, and locally sourced options are gaining traction. BizBash’s 2025 holiday list includes last-minute corporate gifts and cookie boxes, reinforcing that cookies are a recognized corporate-gift format. Food Network’s 2025 holiday gift-basket guide also features craveable cookie boxes as giftable food.

## Host gifts and holiday social behavior
Reddit threads show locals ask for “foodie-quality desserts for host gift,” “holiday gift baskets/boxes,” and office treats. This directly matches Greta’s target woman: she wants to show up with something tasteful, locally sourced, and good enough for discerning friends. Seasonal boxes should be planned around: Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, teacher appreciation, graduations, July 4th/BBQ season, back-to-school, Halloween/Fall, Friendsgiving, Thanksgiving, Christmas/Hanukkah, and Lunar New Year where Asian-inspired flavors such as matcha/macadamia or almond/raspberry can be positioned respectfully.

## Best GTM channels for gifting
1. **Instagram/TikTok drops** with limited pre-order windows and pickup/delivery radius.  
2. **Neighborhood/community groups** in Irvine, Tustin, Costa Mesa, Newport, and school-parent networks.  
3. **Corporate admin/EA outreach** to offices in Irvine Business Complex, Spectrum, UCI area, and medical-device companies.  
4. **Wedding planner and photographer relationships** with sample boxes.  
5. **Real estate agents and mortgage brokers** for closing gifts.  
6. **Holiday pre-order landing page** with fixed quantities and delivery dates.

## Strategic caution
The Cottage Food Act Class A direct-to-consumer structure is an advantage for low overhead but limits scale and channel strategy. OC Health Care Agency and CDPH both state Class A operators are direct-sales only and capped at **$75,000 gross annual sales** in current guidance; if Greta proves corporate/wedding demand, she should plan for Class B/commercial-kitchen scenarios before hitting the cap.
